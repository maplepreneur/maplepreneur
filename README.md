## 🍁 About Me

I'm a passionate **Canadian entrepreneur** and **builder**. I love creating innovative solutions and sharing my journey with the world on X and YouTube.
I run a small web development agency called [Voxel North](https://www.voxelnorth.com) where I help small to medium sized businesses build and scale their online presence. We specialize in building custom web applications, e-commerce stores, and marketing websites using the latest technologies. 

- 🏗️ Current Projects:
    1. [Membase](https://www.membase.ca): A membership management platform for chamer of commerce and associations
    2. [Thymer](https://www.thymer.io): A time and AI tracking for develoepers and teams. 
    3. [Arbora](https://www.arbora.sh): A CLI tool to manage and automate working with multiple AI agents at once.
- 🤖 Always learning and experimenting with AI + Automation
- ♟️ Big fan of chess, hockey and tottenham hotspurs
- ☕ Fueled by coffee, curiosity, and a love of creating things that make a difference.
